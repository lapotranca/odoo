POS and Sales Product Profitability Analysis Report Odoo Apps

Each organisation wants to know that which product are making good profit and which product are not, in the term of profitability for a specific duration. From the large product stock it is very hard to find out profitable product.Using Sales and Point of Sales product profitability analysis report Odoo apps user can identify the product profitability and return rate with the filteration of Day, Week ,Month and Year.

Sales and POS product profitability reports shows daily, weekly, monthly and yearly profitability in percentage also with amount and return rate with the filtration of day, week, month and year. This is one of Sales and POS profits report for sales in Odoo which improve your Product purchase and sales planning.

This Odoo app will work for both Odoo Enterprise and Community Edition.

Sales Product Return Ratio.

POS Product Return Ratio.

Total cost amount and sales amount of point of sale product.

User can see point of sale product profitability analysis report group by day, week, month, year and different time range.
